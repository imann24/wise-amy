Mary Sue

A Mary Sue is a female character who is Super Awesome and Talented Out of Nowhere and All The Boys Like Her. A whole army of women online HATED these characters, but then one day someone said, “hey, doesn’t that describe James Bond and like every male hero ever?” And we all took a moment to grapple with our own misogyny. In modern times, it’s how Nerd Boys(TM) justify hating characters like Rey, because “she’s too good at things it doesn’t make sense, I don’t hate her bc she’s a woman I hate her bc she’s a Mary Sue” despite the fact that Rey has been beating people up w a stick for years and Luke was Super Good at Glow Sticks after one lesson

(That was also what people called self-inserts in fanfics where you’d add a new female character that is everything you wished you were to a franchise. She’d then date all the characters you thought were hot, etc. such a work in the One Direction fandom just became a movie, so... yeah. Weird times.
